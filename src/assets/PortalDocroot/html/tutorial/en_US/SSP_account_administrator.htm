myIATA administrator for a company

Congratulations, you have been appointed administrator of myIATA for your company. Please find as follows the list of your responsibilities and tasks:

My IATA access

myIATA is accessible from www.myista.org.
Select the “Go to myIATA” picture from the right of the page
You will then reach myIATA home page

Initial tasks

Registration

When login myIATA using your temp identification, you will automatically be requested to change your temporary identification:

  • Account name should be your email,
  • Password,
  • Security question
  • Agree on terms and conditions.
Company information

You will then be requested to update your company information. From these screens only some fields can be updated. Contact the Customers Service if you don’t have access to information that must be updated.

Account information

Then you will be requested to fill your contact information.

myIATA home page

You now have access to myIATA

Responsibilities

Maintaining updated your company information

The company information will be used for official communication as for business purpose.

Maintaining updated your account information

Keep your account up to date (account information, password, security question…)

Your email will be used as communication channel between myIATA and you.

Maintain updated the sub-accounts required for your company.

As administrator, you need to create the sub-accounts for your colleges and you need to delete them when they are not required anymore or when employees are living the company.

Notification to new users will be send by email

Sub-account information

Fill the sub-account information

Roles management.

From this screen you can assign/remove roles (rights) to the sub-accounts

Company Visibility

If visibility for other companies is available, From this page you can also assign/remove visibility to the sub-account

Access business information

Access to Ticket Supplies
  • Presentation page
  • Access to business information is granted to you by the IATA administrator.
Application link
  • Order history
Select Track and Trace
  • Access to the business information

Need Help?

  • Each time you are in browsing a page, you can click the Help located in the top right menu.
  • Do you have any problems using the site? Please go to Customer Services section and log an enquiry
  • A tutorial is available from the home page
  • Can’t you see some business information? Please send us a request by filling the form available from contact us.
