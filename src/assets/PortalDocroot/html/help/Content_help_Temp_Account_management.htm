SSP Account Consolidation Help

New Temp Account Management

This screen lets you search for an IATA agency through IATA#, Agency Name, country, city.

Once you key in the information in any of these fields and click on Search, the screen gives the Search Results with all the details of the agency.

View Status: The View Status link tells you whether a particular agency has any accounts setup or not. When you click on View Status for any agency, it takes you to a new window, where you see the details of that agency. The Status field in this new window gives you the details of account setup.

Temp Account Management screen has 2 tabs, Reset Password and New Temporary Account.

Reset Password: When you have forgotten your temporary account password, you can click on Reset Password which lets you reset the password.

New Temporary Account: When you want to create a new temporary account, you can click on this button which lets you create a new temporary account.
