SSP Account Consolidation Help

Account Administration Information

Now you are in the Step 2: Account Administrator Information screen where in you need to enter the account administrator details.

Phone: When you enter the telephone number in the Phone field, make sure to enter the country code in the first box, followed by the area code and the telephone number in the next two boxes.

Fax: when you enter the fax number, follow the same format as you followed while entering the phone number.

Click on Save Changes to Save the account administrator details. Click on Cancel Changes to cancel the changes made on the screen.

When you click on Save Changes, you complete the registration process.
