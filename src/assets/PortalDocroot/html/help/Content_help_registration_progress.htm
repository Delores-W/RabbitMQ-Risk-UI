Registration Progress

You can generate the report on the registration progress of the users on this screen.

Date from: To generate the report, click on the calendar you see in front of Date From field and choose any date. This is the date from which the report is generated.

Date To: Click on the calendar you see in front of Date To field and choose any date. This is the date until which the report is generated.

Country: You can select the country for which you want to generate the report from the drop down menu of Country field.

Status: You can generate reports only for user who have Registered in a specific country during a specified time period. Or generate reports stating all users that are Un-Registered in a specified country. This page will also allow you to generate a complete list of All users.

You can generate the report either in HTML or Excel format.

Click on Generate Report.
