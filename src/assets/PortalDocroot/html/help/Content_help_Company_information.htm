SSP Account Consolidation Help

Company Information

Creation of username and password has 2 steps, Step 1: Company Information and Step 2: Account Administrator Information.You are in the step 1: Company Information screen.

You can fill the details of your company on this screen.

Phone: When you enter the telephone number in the Phone field, make sure to enter the country code in the first box, followed by the area code and the telephone number in the next two boxes.

Fax: Enter fax number only (without country code and area code).

Customer VAT no: Enter the VAT number in this field. This field becomes mandatory if you choose "YES" in Subject to VAT field

Invoice Delivery Method: The invoices will be delivered to you through email. If you want to receive physical copies of the invoice, please uncheck the check box labeled e-invoice.

Once the changes are made, click on Save Changes to save the changes. Click on Cancel Changes if you want to Cancel the changes made on the screen
