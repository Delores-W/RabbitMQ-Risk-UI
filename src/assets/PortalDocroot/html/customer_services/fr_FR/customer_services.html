Reporting Incidents

To report an incident you need to:

  • Contact one of the ITS Service Desks for any incidents concerning the applications (functionality walkthrough
    and IT related issues). If no service desk coverage is available, please contact the Data Center 24/7 Support Line.
  • Provide accurate information if an IT related incident (contact name, application name, impact,
    type of failures (persistent or not), how to reproduce, etc).
