myIATA

Welcome to myIATA! This is the portal where you can access your IATA account's business information and facilitate day-to-day transactions. At this time the following services are available for you:

Ticket Supplies

Here you can follow-up on your ticket automated orders and manual orders and Track & Trace your packages from our warehouse to delivery at your premises.

Invoices

All your IATA electronic invoices can be downloaded here.

In the near future, additional tools will be available to assist you with accreditation and remittance & settlement activities. We will keep you informed about these releases on this page.

For assistance, please use the Help function in the top-right corner of this page or log an enquiry by clicking on Contact Us from the top menu. If it’s the first time you enter myIATA portal we recommend you read our quick tutorials: Tutorial for account administrators and Tutorial for any other myIATA users.
