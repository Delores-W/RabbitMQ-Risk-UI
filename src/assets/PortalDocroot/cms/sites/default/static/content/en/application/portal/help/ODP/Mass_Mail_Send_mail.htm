Mass Mail - Select Template and Send Email

Content:

  • Select the type of template for your mail by selecting the appropriate radio button.
    • Announcement
    • Notification

Action:

  • Select the template format from the drop down menu.
  • The Subject and Body of the Email will be populated according to the Template Selected.
  • You can edit the Subject and Body of the Email as per your requirements.
  • Click on Send Email to send the mail to the group of recipients.
