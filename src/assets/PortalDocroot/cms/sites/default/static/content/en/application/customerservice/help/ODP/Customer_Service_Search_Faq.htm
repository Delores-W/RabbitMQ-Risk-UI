Search FAQs

Purpose:

  • On this screen User can search for FAQs (Frequently asked Questions) in specific categories like Accreditation, CASS procedures and all the categories under user's language, etc.

Actions:

  • Enter the search text in the FAQ text field.
  • Select the categories from the drop down menu.
  • Click on Search to get the desired results.
  • Search Result will display the related questions as hyperlinks.
    • Click on Next to view subsequent search results on the given criteria.
    • Click on Previous to view prior search results on the given criteria.
    • Click on the hyperlinked question to view the answer as a pop-up window.
  • Attached Documents (if any) may be viewed by clicking on the link in the pop-up window.
  • User can Print the answer or Close the window, by clicking on the relevant buttons.
  • User may contact IATA for further clarifications by clicking on the hyperlink - Still looking for an answer and want to contact us ?.
