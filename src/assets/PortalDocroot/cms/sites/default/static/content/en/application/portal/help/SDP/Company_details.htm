Help - myIATA Support Desk Portal

Company Details

Purpose:

  • Displays the Searched criteria separately
  • Most information is read only, few fields can be edited:
    • Email
    • Sales Tax Area Code
    • Invoice Delivery Method (you can make it e-invoice by selecting the check box)
    • Invoice Currency
    • Invoicing Language

Actions:

  • Click on Reset Password to impose the company user to change the password in the next login.
  • Click on Unlock Account to release the account that may be locked.
