Help - myIATA Self Service Portal

View your Enquiries

Purpose:

  • This screen allows users to track their enquiries sent to IATA customer service to closure.
  • User can view solution provided to the query and also add their comments if any.
  • User may view the history of cases and the action taken on them.

Actions:

  • The recent 10 cases are displayed with creation date, subject and Status mentioned.
  • Older cases are listed separately as part of My Case History.
  • Click on the hyperlinked Case Number to view the case details.
  • Click on Write Comments to open a new tab where user can enter the comments.
  • Enter comments and click on Add Comment to attach remarks with the query/solution.
  • Click on Back to go to the previous page to view enquiries.
