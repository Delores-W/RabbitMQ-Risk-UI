Account Management - Mass Trust Assignment

Purpose:

This screen allows building or removing of mass trust for the network of companies belonging to an account.

ODP can search for particular company, view the network and build/remove trust within in that network of companies.

The entire list of companies for that account can be edited to restrict building trust for the entire network.

There are 2 steps involved in Mass Trust Assignment:

  • Step 1: Select Company
  • Step 2: Trust Management

Actions:

Step 1: Select Assignee

  • Select the search group, it can be any one of the below:
    • Agency
    • Airlines
    • Others
    • Person
  • Click on Upload CSV to upload the saved list of assignees in CSV format. An Upload Tab will be displayed.
  • Click on Browse to select the file to upload.
  • Click on Upload to select the new list as the Assignees.

Company Search :

  • Enter any one or more search criteria. Click on Confirm to get the company list.
  • Only the total number of records for the selected search criterion is displayed.
  • Click on Next to begin Mass Trust Assignment on this list of companies under the selected account(s).

To Edit the Company List

  • Click on Download Records to view the entire list of companies for that account to edit it.
  • Save the file in CSV format and make required changes.
  • Click on Upload CSV to upload the saved list of assignees. An Upload Tab will be displayed.
  • Click on Browse to select the file to upload.
  • Click on Upload to select the new list as the Assignees.
  • Click on Next to begin Mass Trust Assignment this uploaded list of companies under the selected account(s).
