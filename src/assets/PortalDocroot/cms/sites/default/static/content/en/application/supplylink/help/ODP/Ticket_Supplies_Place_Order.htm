Help - myIATA Operational Desk Portal

Place a New Order

Purpose:

  • The page allows users to fill forms for placing manual ticket orders.
  • The delivery can be traced and tracked through the View enquiry module under Customer Services.

Actions:

  • Enter the details in all the fields.
  • Click on Submit to send the Order form to IATA.
  • Click on Reset to clear all the fields and again enter values.
