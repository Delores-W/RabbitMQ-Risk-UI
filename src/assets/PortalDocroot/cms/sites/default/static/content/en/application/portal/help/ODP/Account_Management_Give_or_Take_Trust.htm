Account Management - Mass Trust Assignment

Actions:

  • Select the radio button to Give Trust or Take Trust from the Trust Management Tab.
  • Select the radio button to pick Explicit or Implicit from the next Tab.
  • Based on the above selection the Explicit or the Implicit Tab would further appear.
  • Explicit Trust , you can check the relevant direction Check Box (All, Upward or Downward)
    • Click on Remove Trust to eliminate trust from that group of companies.
    • Click on Give Trust to build trust.
    • If user selects Take Trust from the Trust Management Tab, the Give trust button would read as Take trust.
  • Implicit Trust ,
    • The check boxes will not be available.
    • Click on Remove to eliminate trust from that group of companies.
    • Click on Give Trust to build trust.
    • If user selects Take Trust from the Trust Management Tab, the Give trust button would read as Take trust
