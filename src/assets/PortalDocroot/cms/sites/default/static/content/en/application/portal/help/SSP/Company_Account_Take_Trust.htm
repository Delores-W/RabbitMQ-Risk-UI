Company Account - Take Trust

Purpose:

  • On this screen User can view network of companies affiliated to userÂÂs current company.
  • The User can take trust from the network of companies affiliated to users current company.
    • Current company is highlighted in a different color
    • User cannot take trust from companies that are grayed out, where trust is denied .
  • Companies that are registered with IATA have been marked with a green tick mark and the ones which are not registered are denoted by the red colored stop icon

Actions:

  • Select the Check box of the company to take trust .
  • Unselect the check box the company to remove trust.
  • To choose all the companies (which have enabled trust intake) select the check box preceding the header Take Trust
  • Click on Save Changes to update the lists.
  • Click on Cancel Changes to discard all actions.
