Company Information

Purpose:

Company Account enables the access to company information and display related functionalities for the company. SDP Proxy user can view company details and can reset the password or unlock an account for a Company user.
The searched criteria are displayed at the top followed by the entire company information below. Most information is read only, few fields can be edited:

  • Email
  • Sales Tax Area Code
  • Invoice Delivery Method (you can make it e-invoice by selecting the check box)
  • Invoice Currency
  • Invoicing Language

Actions:

  • Click on Reset Password to impose the company user to change the password in the next login.
  • Click on Unlock Account to release the account that may be locked.
