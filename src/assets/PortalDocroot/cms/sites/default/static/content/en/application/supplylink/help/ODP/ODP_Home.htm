ODP Homepage

Purpose:

  • This page shows downtime information if available. Details of the next scheduled downtime(s) (displayed only 24hrs before the schedule time) are displayed with a warning indicating the
    1. Application name
    2. Date
    3. Time
  • Multiple downtimes can also be displayed separately in the same area. The same warning(s) will be displayed on the status bar, 1 hour before the scheduled downtime.
  • The home page enables search for
    1. FAQs (frequently asked questions) about operations and procedures related to BSP, CASS and agent accreditation.
    2. Documents like Manuals, calendars, reporting of Standards Administrative Forms, tutorials and other useful information.
  • User can add frequently referred documents as Favorite Documents . Search for Company/Users link is available on the left panel.

Search for FAQs

  • Enter the search text in the FAQ text field and click on Search to get the desired results.
  • Search Result will display the related questions as hyperlinks
    • Click on Next to view subsequent search results on the given criteria.
    • Click on Previous to view prior search results on the given criteria.
    • Click on the hyperlinked question to view the answer as a pop-up window.
  • Attached Documents (if any) may be viewed by clicking on the link.
  • User can Print the answer or Close the window, by clicking on the relevant buttons.
