New in Invoices!

  • In order to provide you with centralised, permanent access to your IATA invoices, IATA is replacing paper billing with an easy-to-use online service to view invoices for services such as HOTs or BSPLink enhanced fees
  • Instead of receiving traditional paper invoices, from date you will be able to download or print electronic invoices
  • You can view them at any time, avoiding paper circulation and possibly misplaced documents
  • Electronic invoices will now be issued centrally by our Montreal office and no longer by your country offices. It is therefore important that you check the VAT implications that are explained below under the topic "What are reverse charges?"
