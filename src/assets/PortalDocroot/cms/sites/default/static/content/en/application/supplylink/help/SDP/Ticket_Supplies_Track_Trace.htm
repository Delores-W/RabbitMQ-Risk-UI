Status of Ticket Orders

Purpose:

  • Here user can track and trace the status of their ticket orders from the Logistic Provider associated with the user's visible companies.

Actions:

  • Select the Company Name from the drop down menu.
  • The link for Logistic Provider associated with the company will be displayed.
  • Click on the hyperlink to search for the status of your delivery.
