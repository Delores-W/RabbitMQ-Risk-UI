Group Accounts

Purpose:

SSP Users may login myIATA with multiple usernames.
This screen allows SSP user to group all their accounts together so that they can access all accounts (one at a time) by signing in with a single username among the group.

Actions:

  • Provide the Login Credentials below to add that account to the group.
  • Click on Add to Group to insert the new account name in the list of accounts below.
  • Click on Cancel to discard the account details entered.
  • To Remove and account from the group list
    • Select the check box of the desired account to be removed
    • Click on Remove from Group
