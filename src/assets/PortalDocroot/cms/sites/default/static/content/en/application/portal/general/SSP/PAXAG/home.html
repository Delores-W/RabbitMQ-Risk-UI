Welcome to myIATA!

This portal allows you to access all services available for your agency/company, facilitating day-to-day transactions with IATA.

The services you currently have access to

You will see the services available to you in the horizontal navigation bar above. You are granted these services by the myIATA administrator within your company. You may be entitled to other services, see under 'Services available' in 'My account'. To have access to those services, you need to approach your administrator. To see who this person is, follow the link in the left box.
