Report Missing Traffic Documents/CIPs

Purpose:

  • The user can report losses of tickets or CIPs due to burglary, robbery, fire, missing in transits or others on this page.
  • This can be tracked through the View enquiry module under Customer Services.

Actions:

  • General Information will be fetched from the database, these may be edited.
  • Fill the form and click on Print to take a print out the completed form.
  • Sign the form and fax it to the local IATA office.
