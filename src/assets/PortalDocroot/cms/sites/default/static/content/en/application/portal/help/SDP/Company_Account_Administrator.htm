Company Account - Administrator

Purpose:

  • This page displays the list of company Administrators for the user account. Account User can request adding or removing of administrators.

Actions:

  • Click on the Username of the administrator to send a mail to the person .
  • Click on Back to go to the previous menu.
  • Please Contact us (click on the hyperlink) to request adding or removing of administrators.
