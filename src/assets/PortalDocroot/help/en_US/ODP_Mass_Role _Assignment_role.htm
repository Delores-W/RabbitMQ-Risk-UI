Assign Roles

You are in the Step 2: Assign Roles screen where you can Assign Roles to the chosen Assignees.

Assignees List shows you the list of the Assignes you selected. Click on this link to review the Assignees list.

Reselect: You can click on this button to go back to the previous screen and edit the Assignees list.

You can assign roles to either to Super User or All Users by selecting the appropriate radio button in Select Assignees field.

Select Roles to Assign:
Available Roles shows the list of roles available for a particular application. Select the roles from this field and click on the button to select roles for assign or remove operation.

Click on Assign to Assign the roles or Click Remove to Remove Assigned Roles.

Note: Clicking on Remove will remove the selected roles from all users for the selected accounts.
