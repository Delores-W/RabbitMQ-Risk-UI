Sub Account Management – ExternalUser

This screen has details of all the sub user accounts under the company which you have selected. The details include, Username, Name, Address, Phone, Email etc. You can also delete a sub account and unlock a locked user on this screen.

You can click on the sub account name to view the contact information of that sub user.

SUA Admin field indicates whether a particular user is a Sub User Account Administrator. Indicates that the particular user is a SUA Admin.

You can add a new sub user account by clicking on the Add button.

To delete the sub account, select one or more sub accounts from the list and click on Delete button.

To unlock a sub account, select the sub account and click on Unlock Users button.
