IATA Support Desk Portal Help

Support Desk Portal Homepage

The home page has the option of selecting an agency and searching for an agency. You can search an agency using different search criteriaslike IATA#, Country, Agency, Name and City.

You need to key in one or more of this information’s in the respective fields and click on Search. This shows the Search Results which has the desired agency details.

Search Results can have several pages of results. The bottom right corner of Search Results screen has the navigation which takes you to the next page of result when you click on it.

Header contains the following tools:

Name Lastname: Click on this link to view and change your contact information.
Organization :Click on this link to view your company information.
Print this Page: Click on this link to print the content of the page. A Print Dialog Box will be opened, click on Print button to print the page.

SEARCH: Click on this link to search for information related to IATA.
  • Click on SEARCH, type the keywords you want to search in the text feild that appears on the screen, Click on Go to begin searching .
  • This will automatically open up the Google search result webpage and give you the entire search results.
  • When you click on any of these links, you will be taken back to the IATA website and the required information will be displayed.

HELP: If you require help on any of the screens accross the portal, click on this link.
SITEMAP : Click on this link to see the entire structure of the portal.
LOG OUT: Click on this link, to logout of portal and go back to the login screen.

You will be able to see the applications linked to portal as a top level navigation and the functionality available to you as left munu, after selecting a Company.
