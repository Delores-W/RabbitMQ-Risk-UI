Company Visibility

This screen shows you all the companies that you can access.The access will be set by the administrator when the account is setup.

The screen gives the details of all the child companies and the parent company to which the user is associated. The details include, IATA#, Company Name, City, Country, Phone and Email.

The Access? Column tells you whether you have access to the particular child company .
Indicates access is allowed, indicates access denied.

When parent company does not have any child companies, the details of the company that you are associated to will be displayed.
