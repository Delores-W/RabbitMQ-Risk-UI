External Account Management

This screen lets you search for an external user account through IATA#, Agency Name, Country and / or City .

Type in the information you want to search for and click on Search Account. The search results gives you details like IATA code, Company Name, City, Country, Phone and Email. You can click on the company name to view and modify the details of that company.

You can select an account / accounts from the search results and click on Disable Account button at the bottom of the screen. This will disable the chosen company and all users under it. An email notification will automatically be sent to all users.

The Status column in the search results displays the "Locked" status of the account. To unlock an account, select the account and click on the Unlock Account button.

Click on Add New Account to add a new external user.
