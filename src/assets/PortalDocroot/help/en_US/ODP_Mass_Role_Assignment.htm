Mass Role Assignment

Mass Role Assignment process involves 2 steps , Step1: Select Assignee and Step 2: Assign Roles .

You are in the Step1: Select Assignee screen where you can search for accounts and add those Accounts to the Assignees list.

You can search for Accounts based on IATA Code, Company Name, Country or City . Type in the search criteria and click on Search Assignees.

From the search results choose the assignees by selecting the corresponding checkboxes. You can select multiple assignees by navigating through the pages by using the links given below the list.

Note: If you have returned to this screen by clicking on Reselect, you can go back to the Assign Roles screen without changing your Assignees list by clicking on the tab Step 2: Assign Roles.
