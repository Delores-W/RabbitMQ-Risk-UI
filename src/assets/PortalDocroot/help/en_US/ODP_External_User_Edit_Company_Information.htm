Edit Company Information – External user

You can view and Modify the details of a company on this screen.

Phone: Enter the company's phone number in this field. Enter the country code in the first box followed by the area code and the telephone number in the next 2 boxes.

Fax: Enter the company's fax number in this field. When you enter the number, follow the same format as you did to enter the telephone number.

Customer VAT no: Enter VAT number. It becomes necessary to enter the Customer Vat number only if you choose Subject To VAT as “Yes”

Invoice Delivery Method: Choose the delivery method from the dropdown. This is the delivery method which IATA will follow to deliver the invoice to the user. If E-Invoice is selected, the invoice will be delivered by email as a PDF document.

Invoicing language: Click on the drop down to choose the language. The user will receive the invoice in languge selected by him in this field.

Invoicing Currency: Click on the drop down to choose the currency. This indicates the user's preferred currency for any kind of payment to be made to IATA.

Click on Save Changes to save the changes or Cancel Changes to revert back to the last saved information.
