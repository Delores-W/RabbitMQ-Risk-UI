Mass Mailing

Mass Mailing process involves 2 steps , Step1: Select Recipients and Step 2: Select Template & Send Mail .

You are in the Step1: Select Recipients screen where you can search for contacts and add those contacts to the recipients list.

You can search for contacts based on IATA Code, Company Name, Country or City . Type in the search criteria and click on Search Account.

From the searcg results choose the recipients and click on Select to add the recipients to the mailing list and go to the next step of selecting the template and sending the mail.

Note: If you have returned to this screen by clicking on Reselect, you can go back to the Send Mail screen without changing your contact list by clicking on the tab Step 2: Select Template & Send Mail.
