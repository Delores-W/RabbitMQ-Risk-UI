IATA Support Desk Portal Help

Agency Details

This screen, lets you view and update the company information you are working on.

The fields Company name, Parent Company, Address of the Company, IATA code and the Type of Company cannot be modified.

Phone: When you enter the telephone number in this field, make sure to enter the country code in the first box followed by the area code and the telephone number in the next 2 boxes.

Fax: When you enter the fax number in the Fax field, follow the same format as you did to enter the telephone number.

Email: This field should have the email address of the company.

Customer VAT no: Enter your VAT number.

Invoice Delivery Method: This field indicates your choice of receiving the invoice confirmation. If you want the invoice confirmation through the email, then click and place a check mark in front of eInvoice.

Click on Save Changes to update the Company Information. Click on Cancel Settings to revert back to the previously saved Information .
