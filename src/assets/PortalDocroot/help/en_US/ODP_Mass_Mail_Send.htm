Select Template & Send Mail

You are in the Step 2: Select Template & Send Mail screen where you can select a template and send the mail to the chosen recipients.

Recipients List shows you the list of the recipients you selected. Click on this link to review the recipients list.

Reselect: You can click on this button to go back to the previous screen and edit the recipients list.

You can send mails either to Company or Super User or All User by selecting the appropriate radio button in Select Recipients field.

Select Template: Choose a template from the drop down menu .Once you choose a particular template, it automatically populates the Subject and Body field. However both of these fields are editable.

Click on Send Mail to send the mail to all the recipients.
